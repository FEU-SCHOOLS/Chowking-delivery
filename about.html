Our Story

Everyday Chinese sarap since 1985

From Humble Beginnings

Chowking was founded in 1985 with the goal of combining the convenience of fast food with the richness of Chinese cuisine. Over the years, we've grown from a small food outlet to one of the leading Chinese-style quick-service restaurants in the Philippines.

Our Mission

To bring the joy of great-tasting Chinese food to everyone. Whether you're dining in, taking out, or ordering online - we serve comfort food made with love, tradition, and Filipino warmth.

Part of a Bigger Family

Chowking is proud to be a part of the Jollibee Foods Corporation (JFC) family - the largest and fastest-growing Asian restaurant company in the world since 2000.

“Delighting customers with every bite, one meal at a time.”
