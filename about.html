About Us

Your favorite Chinese fast-food chain in the Philippines.

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Chowking is known for combining Chinese cuisine with fast food. It is the first and largest Chinese fast-food chain in the Philippines. It started its operations with a single branch and has since grown to hundreds of stores nationwide and even branches overseas, including the United States, United Arab Emirates, and Qatar.

Chowking offers a diverse menu that blends traditional Chinese dishes with modern fast-food convenience. Popular items include its delicious Chao Fan (fried rice), Lauriat meals which are complete rice meals with various viands, dim sum, noodles, and refreshing Halo-Halo. The brand continually innovates its menu to cater to the evolving tastes of its customers while staying true to its Chinese heritage.

"Bringing Chinese Sarap to every Filipino home, one meal at a time!"
